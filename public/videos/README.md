# Video Assets

Place your video files here:

- `hero-bg.mp4` - Main hero background video (recommended: 1080p, H.264, 15-30 seconds loop)
- Additional video content for sections

## Recommended Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum
- **Bitrate**: 5-10 Mbps for quality/size balance
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds for loops
- **Audio**: Remove or mute for background videos
